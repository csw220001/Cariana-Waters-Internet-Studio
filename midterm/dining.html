Sweet Treats!

Marsha's Tavern

Newly famous thanks to her adoptive
sister's rise to power, the tavern of
miss Marsha Mallow is a staple
of Peppermint Forest cuisine. She
is well known for her famous hot chocolate.

Sugar-Top Stop

Sugar-Top Stop is a small, isolated inn/restaurant
at the east entrance of the Gumdrop Mountains'
main trail. A warm respite from the
chill of the mountain pass. It's a relatively new
location, having been funded in part by the
Queen herself in an effort to populate the
mountains. Their world-famous
gumdrop gumbo is to die for
according to Queen Merry,
who is a known foodie.

Muffin Madness

Don't be fooled by the name,
this extravagant, bustling cafe sells
more than simple muffins. Though
their muffins are indeed their staple,
Muffin Madness sells all manner of
baked goods to the people
of the Candy Queendom.

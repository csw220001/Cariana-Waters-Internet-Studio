Activities!

Sweet Harvest Festival!

The Sweet Harvest Festival is an annual festivity celebrating the candy-corn harvest in the queendom. There's food, music, games, and even a speech from the queen herself! At the end is a beautiful fireworks show that you won't want to miss.

Tour the Queen's Palace!

Queen Merry invites you to tour her large yet cozy palace.

Fallen Heroes' Memorial

Honor all those who suffered and died under King Kandy's reign, and see the visage of a cookie who gave their life for us at the Fallen Heroes' Memorial.

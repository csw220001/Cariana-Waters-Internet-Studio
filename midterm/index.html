Welcome to Karamela!

Karamela is a land of abundant sweets of all kinds. It is a land that strife no longer ravages, thanks to the efforts of our benevolent new Goddess-Queen. Karamela is a land where people need not work to live, and instead can pursue their passions. Human children come to visit on occasion, replenishing our land with their pure sweetness.

Karamela was once ruled by Queen Icine and her family, until a horrible tragedy occured. Queen Merry saved the land from a horrible usurper and took her rightful place back on the throne!

Did you know? The old queen's palace is sunken at the bottom of the Syrup Lake!

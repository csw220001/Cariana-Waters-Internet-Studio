Domain Name Ideas

  • wondersoftheworld.net
  • manywonders.org
  • findindie.org

Discussion

My neighbor and I discussed my three ideas after checking to make sure they were available. We concluded a few things. For one, wondersoftheworld is hard to read. It looks like Wonder-Softhe-World manywonders looks good, reads clearly, and is a nice domain name. Findindie is the best though. Not only is it easy to read and remember, but it also tells the user exactly what the site is for.

Decision

Based on our discussion, I've decided to move forward with findindie.org, as I think it is the clearest and easiest to remember. It details the mission of my website and being easy to remember will make it more easily accessible to users.

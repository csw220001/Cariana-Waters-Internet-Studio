My Dodgeball Team

This is my dodgeball team: The God Squad. Led by legendary god of chaos Bugs Bunny, this is a team that trounces all opponents thanks to their diversified skillset and ace player. Brawn, dexterity, magic, cartoon logic? They've got it all.

Grog Strongjaw

Grog Strongjaw of Vox Machina is this group's main brawn. He packs a force into his throws unlike any other thanks to his immense strength and magical gauntlets. He's not the smartest or the most agile but he makes up for it with his big muscles and bigger heart.

Raven

Agile and magically skilled, Raven is a frightening adversary on the field. She's an aggressive and determined player, abetted by her dark spellcraft.

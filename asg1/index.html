Part 3

For my final project I'd like to create a website that promotes indepentent works such as indie animated shows, indie games, and self-published books.
The website will be a platform where these works will come to light easier and perhaps garner a greater audience than they might otherwise find in the
seas of media that are Youtube, Steam, and Tiktok. The site will have the ability to allow creators to upload a preview or trailer of their work, as well
as links to it, and allow users to filter media by medium. I want there to be two feeds: one feed of the most popular or trending pieces of independent
media, and another that is purely random and refreshes with the page.
